Image Dimensions

Stable Diffusion was trained for base dimensions around 512px (SD 1.5) and 768px (SD 2/2.1).
While it's not necessary to stay in that line of multiples of 128, it's a good starting point.

Some of the common ratios in SD dimensions:

Square 1:1 512 × 512
Photo 2:3 512 × 768
Photo 3:4 512 × 683
Social Media 4:5 512 × 640
Standard Monitor 16:9 911 × 512
Monitor 16:10 820 × 512
UltraWide Monitor 21:9 1195 × 512
